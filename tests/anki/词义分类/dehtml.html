prep. 1 在…的下面；到…的下面；通过…的下面：
  1. to look under the bed 看床底下
  2. a tunnel under the ground 一条地道
  3. She was lying under the umbrella. 她躺在遮阳伞下。
  4. to place the ladder under the window 把梯子立在窗户下面
  5. The dog squeezed under the gate and ran into the road.狗从门底下钻出去，跑到大路上去了。
  6. He went under an arch. 他从一座拱门下面走过。
  7. A path runs under the trees. 一条小路在树下延伸。
 2 在…的表面下；由…覆盖着：
  1. He was under several feet of water. 他在水下好几英尺。
  2. I was wearing a sweater under the jacket. 我那时在夹克里面穿着一件毛衣。
adv. 1 在下面：
  1. He pulled up the covers and crawled under. 他揭开被子钻到里面。
 2 在水面下：
  1. He stayed under for more than a minute. 他潜水一分多钟。
  2. The boat was going under fast. 小船正迅速下沉。
